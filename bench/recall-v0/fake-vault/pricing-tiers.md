---
name: Quillfly pricing tiers (2026-Q2)
type: reference
---

Three tiers, priced per drone per month:

- **Starter** — €49/drone/month. Up to 10 drones. Community support only.
- **Fleet** — €39/drone/month. 11–100 drones. Business-hours email support.
- **Enterprise** — Custom. 101+ drones. 24/7 phone + dedicated SRE liaison. Minimum annual commit €75,000.

**Annual prepay discount:** 15% off Starter and Fleet, negotiated separately for Enterprise.

**Free tier removed 2026-01-15** — replaced with 30-day Starter trial. Existing free-tier customers grandfathered until 2026-06-30.
