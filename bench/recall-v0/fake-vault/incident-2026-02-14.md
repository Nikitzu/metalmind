type: incident
date: 2026-02-14

**Summary:** 2026-02-14 11:23 UTC, 412 drones across 7 operators received a faulty emergency-land command and set down wherever they were, including over water and in restricted airspace.

**Root cause:** a stale feature flag `emergency_land_all = true` left in the config after a drill on 2026-02-12 was not cleared. The flag evaluator cached for 48 hours and fired again at the next config reload.

**MTTR:** 34 minutes (detection 8 min, rollback 26 min).

**Fixes shipped:**

1. Emergency-land flags now require a TTL and auto-expire after 4 hours (QF-INC-58-F1, shipped 2026-02-15).
2. All destructive flags require two-person confirmation before eval (QF-INC-58-F2, shipped 2026-02-18).
3. Drill procedures now include a mandatory post-drill flag-audit step (runbook updated 2026-02-17).

**Cost:** €118,400 in drone recovery + goodwill credits to operators. Zero injuries.

Ownership of postmortem: Dmitri.
